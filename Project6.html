Restaurant Menu Design

The menu design is for what would be a modern fine-dining restaurant called The Alla Moda. These are the Italian words for upscale or trendy. You would find this restaurant in San Francisco, California on Market Street.

The final design product is a menu design that is 8” x 10” single page and 10" x 16” double page spread when the menu is opened. Included items are completed digital writing, logo designs, brand identity colors, and graphics.
